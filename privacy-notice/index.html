Summer Together privacy policy

This privacy notice explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you participate in the Summer Together contest.

We designed the online challenges with a Privacy First approach. Most challenges are fully anonymous and cannot be traced back to you. When we do collect information that can be considered personal data, our privacy policy explains how we treat this data securely and in compliance with GDPR.

1. Who is responsible for your data?

This contest is organized by Better Together. You can contact us by sending a message to sh-ne-nl-bettertogether@cevalogistics.com or by filling in our contact form.

2. What data do we collect?

  • Your uploaded image(s) and/or anecdotes
  • Your name (if provided)
  • Any additional information you include in the form

We do not use cookies, trackers, or analytics tools in any of our contests.

3. Why do we collect this data?

We collect your data to:

  • Run the Summer Together competition
  • Showcase selected entries (if consent is given)

We do not use automated decision-making or profiling based on your personal data.

4. Legal basis for processing

We rely on your explicit consent to collect and use your personal data for this contest. You may withdraw this consent at any time by contacting us (see Section 7).

5. How is your data stored?

We use Tally.so to collect submissions for the Summer Together competition. Tally.so processes and stores this data on our behalf. Better Together therefore remains the data controller.

All data submitted via Tally.so is encrypted and stored securely within the EU in compliance with GDPR. Tally.so uses subprocessors to host and store the challenges and the information submitted through them.

For the purposes of the competition leaderboard, submissions are also exported and stored on our company-regulated SharePoint environment, which is protected by strict access controls and security measures in line with our companyâ€™s data protection policies. This data is pseudonymized to protect your identity by removing direct identifiers such as photo URLs and using randomized respondent IDs. Pseudonymization means that identifying information is replaced with codes or identifiers, reducing the risk of directly identifying you.

Access to SharePoint data is protected by multi-factor authentication and role-based access controls to ensure only authorized personnel can view or manage your data.

6. How long do we keep your data?

Your data will be deleted within 21 days after the contest ends, unless you withdraw your consent earlier. If you withdraw your consent, we will delete your personal data as soon as possible, in accordance with your request (see Section 7 for details on your rights and how to contact us).

However, please note that our data processor retains deleted data for up to 90 days in backups, after which data is permanently deleted. During this period your data is not accessible or used within the context of the Summer Together competition.

Pseudonymized data in SharePoint is currently not set for deletion, unless you request the deletion of your data with us.

7. Your rights

Under the GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Correct any inaccuracies
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

To exercise your rights or ask questions about this policy, please contact: sh-ne-nl-bettertogether@cevalogistics.com or or use our contact form.

8. Complaints

If you have any concerns about how we handle your personal data, please contact us by sending us a message. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Dutch supervisory authority:

9. Changes to this privacy notice

We reserve the right to update or change this privacy notice at any time. If we make significant changes, we will notify participants through a changelog on this page. We encourage you to review this privacy notice periodically to stay informed about how we protect your data.
