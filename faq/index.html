Frequently asked questions

You'll find the answers to the most common questions below!

Can we really do any team activity?
The grand prize is only for permanent staff and must be approved by HR, like any other team activity. However, everybody--including flex employees--will receive a summer gadget and a food booster!
How can I participate?
Scan the QR card, check the canteens and be present at the entrance on the mentioned timeslots. Everything will be clear there!
To which team do I belong?
The teams are divided by site, department and shift. For operational teams, your team will match your own department and shift. Some staff departments have been merged in places to create more equal teams. You're in the same team throughout the whole event. Still not sure? A full overview will be shared from Tuesday 17 June onwards!
My team is much smaller than others. How is this fair?
To make sure all teams have a fair chance at winning the main prize, we are placing a limit on the amount of points that can be won per team through individual challenges. That way, bigger teams do not have the advantage of being able to submit more challenges.
How will I know if I won?
The winners of Summer Together 2025 will be announced during the Closing Ceremony on June 26 and thereafter on the narrowcasting. Have you won? We'll contact your team soon to award the grand prize!
I'm a flex employee. Why can I not win the main prize?
In line with our internal policy surrounding team events, the main prize is limited to permanent staff. However, this is still a team effort! We encourage flex employees to also participate in the competition. Online challenges

How can I participate in the online challenges?
Scan the QR code on the poster or QR card you received. This will take you to our linktree where you can find the links to all online challenges!
How often can I fill in the online challenges?
Each challenge can be filled in once.
What happens to my data?
We set up the online challenges in the principle of Privacy First. Most challenges are fully anonymous and cannot be traced back to you. Your photo submission, meme submission or memory submission are treated as personally identifiable information.

We store this safely within the EU in compliance with GDPR. Your data is only used for the purposes of the Summer Together competition, will not be shared with others and will be securily deleted 21 days after the competition ends. You can at all times request us to delete your data by withdrawing your consent by contacting us through the "Shoot us a message" button above. Be sure to check out our privacy policy for full details.
