Frequently asked questions

You'll find the answers to the most common questions below!

Can we really do any team activity?
The grand prize is only for permanent staff and must be approved by HR, like any other team activity. However, everybody--including flex employees--will receive a summer gadget and a food booster!
How can I participate?
Scan the QR card, check the canteens and be present at the entrance on the mentioned timeslots. Everything will be clear there!
To which team do I belong?
The teams are divided by site, department and shift. For operational teams, your team will match your own department and shift. Some staff departments have been merged in places to create more equal teams. You're in the same team throughout the whole event. Still not sure? A full overview will be shared from Tuesday 17 June onwards!
My team is much smaller than others. How is this fair?
To make sure all teams have a fair chance at winning the main prize, we are placing a limit on the amount of points that can be won per team through individual challenges. That way, bigger teams do not have the advantage of being able to submit more challenges.
